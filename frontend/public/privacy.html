Privacy Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This policy explains how Umovu Technology ("we", "us") handles personal information when you use Crowds ("the Service"). We process personal information in line with South Africa's Protection of Personal Information Act (POPIA).

1. Information we collect

  • Account data — your email address and authentication details, handled by our authentication provider, Supabase.
  • Billing data — your subscription status and payment records. Card payments are processed by Paystack; we do not see or store your full card number.
  • Usage data — the scenarios you submit and the runs you create, so we can operate the Service and show you your history.
  • Technical data — basic logs (e.g. request times, errors) needed to keep the Service running and secure.

2. How we use it

  • To provide and operate the Service and your account.
  • To process subscription payments and enforce plan limits.
  • To maintain security, prevent abuse, and debug problems.
  • To respond to support requests.

We do not sell your personal information.

3. Who we share it with

We share data only with the processors needed to run the Service:

  • Supabase — authentication and database hosting.
  • Paystack — payment processing.
  • AI model providers — only the scenario text you submit for a run is sent for processing; we do not send your account or payment details.

4. Retention

We keep account and run data for as long as your account is active. You can ask us to delete your account and associated data, subject to records we must keep for legal or accounting reasons.

5. Your rights

Under POPIA you may ask to access, correct, or delete your personal information, and object to certain processing. To exercise these rights, reach us through our contact page.

6. Security

We use industry-standard measures — encrypted connections, access controls, and trusted sub-processors — to protect your information. No system is perfectly secure, but we work to keep your data safe.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above reflects the latest version.

8. Contact

Privacy questions can be sent via our contact page.
