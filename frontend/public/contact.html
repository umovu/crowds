About the business

Crowds is a subscription software-as-a-service (SaaS) product operated by Umovu Technology, a technology business based in South Africa. We sell access to a web application; we do not sell physical goods and we do not hold customer funds.

What the service does. Customers describe a scenario in plain language — a policy, a public announcement, a marketing message, or a product idea — and Crowds runs that scenario through a simulated population of AI agents grounded in South African socio-economic context. The customer receives a written report summarising the likely range of public or market reactions, including objections, supportive views, and adoption barriers. It is a decision-support and research tool used to rehearse a message before it goes live.

Who buys it. Our customers are policy and communications teams, startup founders and product teams, market researchers, and consultants who want fast directional feedback before committing to a launch or announcement.

What customers pay for. Access is sold as a monthly subscription:

  • Free — one panel run per month, at no charge, so customers can evaluate the tool.
  • Pro — R80 per month for unlimited panels and full multi-round simulations, including reports and follow-up interviews.

How payments work. Paid subscriptions are billed monthly in South African Rand (ZAR) and processed securely by Paystack. We accept card payments. We do not store full card details — these are handled by Paystack. Customers can cancel at any time; see our Refund & Cancellation policy and Terms of Service.
