Refund & Cancellation Policy

Last updated: 25 June 2026

This policy applies to the Crowds Pro subscription, operated by Umovu Technology and billed at R80 per month in South African Rand (ZAR) through Paystack.

1. Free plan

The Free plan involves no payment and nothing to cancel or refund. You can stop using it at any time.

2. Cancelling Pro

You can cancel your Pro subscription at any time from your account. When you cancel:

  • Your subscription stops renewing, so you are not billed again.
  • Your Pro access continues until the end of the month you have already paid for.
  • After that, your account reverts to the Free plan.

3. Refunds

Subscription payments are non-refundable on a pro-rata basis — cancelling part-way through a billing month does not generate a partial refund, because you keep full access for the rest of that month.

If you were charged in error (for example, billed after a confirmed cancellation, or a duplicate charge), contact us and we will investigate and refund any incorrect charge.

4. How to request

To cancel, use the account/billing controls in the app. For a billing query or a charged-in-error refund, reach us through our contact page with the email on your account and the approximate date of the charge.

5. Payment processor

Payments and any approved refunds are handled through Paystack. Refunds are returned to the original payment method and the timing depends on your bank or card provider.
